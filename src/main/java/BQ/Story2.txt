
PA Expansion 项目 - 前端 Marketplace Filter 交互冲突类 STAR 故事（优化版）

一、中文 STAR 故事（贴合真实经历，无虚构）

1. Situation（背景）

在 PAU Expansion 项目中，我负责前端核心交互开发，核心需求是为 mult marketplace 场景添加 marketplace 筛选组件（widget）。
不同于 JP 地区的 single marketplace 场景，PAU 是多市场模式，需要支持多市场筛选功能。
项目初期，PM 和 UX Designer（UXD）就这个筛选组件的交互逻辑，提出了一套非常完善但过于复杂的方案，而项目 deadline 非常紧张，必须按时交付才能保障 PAU 地区业务顺利上线。

2. Task（任务）

我需要负责这个 marketplace 筛选组件的前端开发，确保组件交互流畅、满足业务筛选需求，同时配合团队完成整体项目交付，不延误 PAU 地区的上线计划。

3. Action（行动）

PM 和 UXD 提出的交互方案过于复杂，不仅要求支持多市场切换、模糊搜索、批量筛选，还需添加自定义筛选模板、筛选历史记录等附加功能，开发工作量极大。
结合紧张的 deadline，我判断按此方案开发，必然无法按时交付，还可能影响整个 PAU 上线进度。
于是我第一时间提出了不同意见，明确告知 PM 和 UXD：当前方案开发量过大，无法在 deadline 前完成，且复杂的交互反而可能增加用户操作成本。
随后我结合 PAU 地区用户使用习惯，提出了简化方案——先实现核心筛选功能（基础市场切换、精准筛选），保证组件可用、满足业务核心需求，待项目上线后，再通过 quick follow-up 迭代，补充他们提出的复杂交互功能。
期间我和 PM、UXD 进行了多次沟通，展示了简化方案的原型，测算出两种方案的开发周期差异（复杂方案需10个工作日，简化方案仅需4个工作日），强调延误上线对 PAU 业务的影响，
最终三方达成一致：本次上线优先实现简化方案，后续快速迭代补充复杂功能。
确定方案后，我快速推进开发，同步每日进度给 PM 和 UXD，及时反馈开发过程中的问题，同时为后续迭代预留扩展接口，确保后续复杂功能能顺利落地。

4. Result（结果）

我们成功在 deadline 前完成了简化版筛选组件的开发和上线，保障了 PAU 地区业务顺利启动，没有出现任何延误。
简化版组件完全满足核心筛选需求，用户使用反馈良好，无交互卡顿或操作不便的问题。
项目上线后，我们快速启动 follow-up 迭代，在1周内完成了 PM 和 UXD 最初提出的复杂交互方案，既满足了业务长期需求，也赢得了 PM 和 UXD 的信任，后续他们提出新需求时，都会先和我沟通技术可行性。

二、英文 STAR 故事（口语优化版，流畅自然，适配面试语气）

Situation

During the PA EU Expansion project, I was responsible for the front-end development of a marketplace filter widget.
Unlike the JP region which only had a single marketplace, EU was a multi-marketplace scenario, so we needed a filter to support multi-market selection.
At the beginning, our PM and UX Designer proposed a very comprehensive but overly complex interaction plan for this widget.

Task

My task was to develop this marketplace filter widget, ensure its usability and smooth interaction,
and coordinate with PM and UX Designer to deliver it on time, so that the EU regional business could launch as scheduled.

Action

I soon realized that the interaction plan proposed by PM and UX Designer was too complex—it included multi-market switching, fuzzy search,
batch filtering, custom filter templates, and search history records. Given the tight project deadline, I knew we couldn’t finish it on time
if we followed this plan. So I disagreed with their proposal directly.

I explained my viewpoint clearly: the core need of the EU business was to have a usable filter to support multi-market selection,
and the complex interaction they proposed was not a must for the initial launch.
Then I showed them a prototype of my simplified plan, which only focused on the core functions—basic market switching and precise filtering.
I also compared the development cycle of the two plans: the complex plan would take 10 working days, while the simplified one only needed 4 days.
I emphasized the impact of delaying the EU launch if we stuck to the complex plan.

After several rounds of discussion, PM and UX Designer agreed to my simplified plan. I then accelerated the development, updated my progress to them every day,
and reserved expansion interfaces for the follow-up complex functions. I also communicated with the back-end team to ensure the data support matched the simplified filter function.

Result

We successfully delivered the simplified marketplace filter widget before the deadline, which ensured the smooth launch of EU regional business without any delay.
The simplified widget fully met the core filtering needs, and users gave positive feedback on its simplicity and usability.
Within one week after the launch, we completed the follow-up iteration and implemented all the complex interaction functions proposed by PM and UX Designer.
This process also earned me the trust of PM and UX Designer—they would consult me on technical feasibility before proposing new interaction plans later.

三、匹配的 Leadership Principles（LP）及解析

1. Disagree & Commit（亚马逊/Zuora/Booking 必考）
Matching Point: I disagreed with PM and UX Designer on the complexity of the filter widget’s interaction plan.
I clearly expressed my viewpoint, provided data support (development cycle comparison), proposed a feasible alternative,
and finally reached an agreement with them.
This fully reflects the Disagree & Commit principle—expressing disagreement respectfully and committing to the final decision.

2. Ownership（主人翁意识）
Matching Point: When I found the original plan was not feasible, I took the initiative to disagree, proposed a simplified solution,
and took full responsibility for the development and delivery of the filter widget, ensuring it met the core needs and the project deadline.
This shows my strong ownership.

3. Deliver Results（交付成果）
Matching Point: Faced with a tight deadline, I proposed a practical simplified plan, coordinated with PM and UX Designer to reach an agreement,
and successfully delivered the widget on time, ensuring the smooth launch of EU regional business. This reflects my ability to deliver results under pressure.
